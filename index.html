Who I am

I observe systems.
I make them better.

Curiosity has always led the way. It took me everywhere. Curious, new and figuring things out. It took me to multiple countries and internships at the UN, the Danish Refugee Council, the Stockholm Environment Institute and now Inter IKEA Group, alongside degrees at four universities across four countries. Every experience has taught me something different and something real about how people operate and how organisations work. It showed me where coordination breaks down, and how people, not systems, are always at the centre of any real solution.
