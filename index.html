Who I am

I understand systems.
I make them better.

I came to Sweden in 2014 on a scholarship. Curious, new and figuring things out. My curiosity has taken me to multiple countries and internships at the UN, the Danish Refugee Council, the Stockholm Environment Institute and now Inter IKEA Group, alongside degrees at four universities across four countries. Every experience has taught me something different about how organisations work, where coordination breaks down, and how people, not systems, are always at the centre of any real solution.
